***OUR LOCATION***


Seaside South Park Hotel is a hidden gem on the emerald isle of Sri Lanka.
Located just 20 min south of the capital city,Colombo.Seaside South Park is an oasis in an otherwise bustling city.
Hidden away among coconut groves and lush beach vegetation, Seaside South Park has always kept nature in mind and
has long been considered by regulars as a hideaway on the beaches of Mount Lavinia.












***OUR HISTORY***


The Hotel originally opened in 1864, borrowing its name from the charming expanse of ground known as Seaside South Park,
which stretches for a kilometer by the side of the Hotel, along the coast, where, in Victorian times,
it was a popular place to take a walk in the sea air, or a ride in a horse-drawn carriage.
The Hotel was built section by section, the first being the central area that leads to the awe inspiring Ballrooms,
followed by the South and North wings respectively.
It was in 1894, it became a two-storey luxury hotel, with the help of the most famous architect of the time, Edward Skinner,
while between 1903 and 1909, Seaside South Park Hotel Company continued to buy up land that would allow the hotel to expand to its present size.
